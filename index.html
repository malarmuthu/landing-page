Hiking the US National Parks

This website gives details of some of my favorite National Parks that I have hiked in the US.
I hope you enjoy it. Sign up to get more updates.

Let's have a look at some of the beautiful National parks which is a must visit

Yosemite has many trials, I personally loved hiking Taft point. Its a breath taking view from here. It's a short doable hike. There is half Dome hike which has some amazing views and a very streous hike. We also did the vernal falls hike.

There is no one who would not love to watch the beautiful grand canyon. Its huge and beautiful. We did not hike much here. There are many trials which even includes overnight camping. We did smaller easier hikes, as we had to cover anteloppe canyons during our visit.

Rocky Mountains is breath taking, has some amazing views and very strenous hikes. If you are an avid hikers, take all necessary precautions before heading here. Enjoy some breath taking views. We did Emerald Lake and Dream Lake trials.

Smoky Mountains is one of the most visited National parks in the East. There are many trials to choose and pick the one of your pace. I have personally enjoyed Alum Cave trials which leads to Mt LaConte.It's a very strenous hike with some amazing views.

I took the one less travelled by, and that has made all the difference -Robert Frost
